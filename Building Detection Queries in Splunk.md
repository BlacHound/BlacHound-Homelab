### Telementry:
the process of recording and transmitting the readings of an instrument.

Splunk is a giant warehouse of raw telementry. Process executions, login attempts, file writes and rewrites, network connections and registry changes. This all involves Data Collection and the monitoring of activity happening where data lives and moves.

As a SOC analyst I need splunk to look for certain anomalies within the logs that it has acquired and alert me to it's findings. This is called detection, and to Query means to ask/question about something and to check its validity or accuracy.

and as a SOC analyst, my job is to question logs:
-  1. Is this normal?
   2. If not normal, is it malicious?
   3. If it is malicious, how bad is it?

 Detection Queries are how we use splunk to automate the first 2 questions, so I can focus on the 3rd question.
